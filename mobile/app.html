Angelica

This classy cocktail lounge and restaurant boasts a scenic rooftop terrace above the hubbub of the city centre. The bartenders will whip up anything to your taste, or you can choose from an exciting selection of signature cocktails.

angelica-restaurant.com

Distrikt

Distrikt brings together music, art, food and drinks in quirky style. Hidden in a basement off Duncan Street, this unique bar has been hailed as one of Leeds' finest. The venue hosts lively and eclectic performances by reknowned DJs.

distrikt.co.uk

Friends of Ham

Tucked away in a small street by station, Friends of Ham offers a relaxed spot to enjoy a craft beer and a plate of cheese and charcuterie after a spot of shopping. Sit by the window and watch the passers-by as you catch up with friends.

friendsofham.com

Hifi

Hifi hosts regular funk, disco and motown nights, gigs and comedy nights. Finish your weekend there at the Sunday Joint and discover a new band for free, or check out their events page to catch up and coming acts in an intimate setting.

thehificlub.co.uk

Nation of Shopkeepers

The burgers at Nation are some of the finest around and the wide variety of toppings means there's something for everyone. Free music, a great selection of beer and a courtyard make this a great destination on a sunny day or for a chilled night out with friends.

anationofshopkeepers.com

Oporto

It's all about the music at Oporto, with live gigs throughout the week, mainly showcasing local musicians, and a focus on acoustic sets. The atmosphere is intimate, and a good selection of drinks and American-style fast food easily make this one of the best bars on Call Lane.

oportobar.co.uk

Sela bar

Located on New Briggate, this dimly-lit underground jazz bar is a great spot to enjoy live music. Try one of their speciality cocktails and if you feel peckish, grab yourself a pizza. It gets very crowded so if you want a table, be sure to get down early.

selabar.com

Smokestack

Find the entrance to Smokestack on Call Lane and head up the stairs to find this quaint speakeasy-style jazz bar on two floors. The lower floor has a cosy seating area, while on the top floor you'll find regular salsa classes and lively band nights.

smokestackleeds.co.uk

Wharf Chambers

Here you will find everything from cool indie bands to spoken word nights and yoga classes. The atmosphere is hippyish and relaxed, and it's seriously cheap. It's a co-op, so membership is necessary to be let in - but for only £1, it's worth the money.

wharfchambers.org
